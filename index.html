about thrifty nyc

Welcome to Thrifty NYC, your ultimate guide to unlocking the free and discounted experiences in the heart of New York City! In a city known for its bustling energy and diverse cultural offerings, we understand the importance of enjoying all that it has to offer without breaking the bank. Thrifty NYC is here to be your go-to resource for budget-friendly adventures and exclusive deals that make your time in the Big Apple memorable without draining your wallet.

Discover a plethora of events, activities, and attractions as well as insider tips on snagging discounts for popular attractions, dining, and entertainment. From outdoor festivals to art exhibitions, hidden parks to wallet-friendly dining options, we've got you covered. Join us in embracing the thrifty spirit of NYC and embark on a journey that proves you don't need to break the bank to experience the best of New York!

new york tips

free activities

Staten Island Ferry 

The Staten Island Ferry gives you amazing close up views of the Statue of Liberty. It runs 24/7 every 30 minutes (apart from every 15 minutes weekdays during rush hours)

Walking Tours 

Enjoy a selection of different walking tours lasting from 2-6 hours covering various areas of New York (but not Manhattan). Free tours but you can leave a tip.

Park 

Little Island is an artificial island park just off Pier 54 where you can have an audio tour, enjoy the landscape, take a yoga class, relax and watch the sunset, or attend an event.

Films 

Enjoy an outdoor film at one of the many Manhattan parks from May onwards.

Beer Tour 

Brookyln Brewery offers free tours every Sunday hourly from 1-6pm. No tasting is included but you can buy a drink in the bar.

discounted activities

Shopping 

Macy’s (the largest store in the USA) offers a Visitor’s Savings pass with 10% discount on many products. Collect in store on the mezzanine level (you need ID too).

Theatre 

Get up to 50% off a Broadway theatre ticket by going to a ticket booth on the day of the show. (You'll need to queue). Also Off Broadways shows are discounted.

Museums 

Many museums offer free or pay what you wish entry weekly (often they are closed on a Monday). Look at museum websites for more details.

Food 

For $20 the Eat and Play card offers discounts on food, attractions, shopping and shows.

Drinks 

Many New York bars off happy hours with big savings.
